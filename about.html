HELLO

My name is Aidan (he/him) and I'm a visual artist. I have a passion
for photography, video, and programming, hoping to find an
intersection between the sports and creative worlds.

I was born and raised in Southern Ohio before I moved to
New York City to attend New York Univeristy's Tisch School
of the Arts, studying Interactive Media Arts and Film Production.

I am a fan of early mornings, good podcasts, creating content,
tossing the frisbee, Ohio sports, and great, local coffee.
